## Run a seed on akash

```yaml
---
version: "2.0"

services:
  osmosis:
    image: ghcr.io/notional-labs/tinyseed:latest
    env: 
     - ID=bostrom
     - SEEDS=d0518ce9881a4b0c5872e5e9b7c4ea8d760dad3f@85.10.207.173:26656
    expose:
      - port: 8080
        to:
          - global: true
      - port: 6969
        to:
          - global: true
profiles:
  compute:
    osmosis:
      resources:
        cpu:
          units: 1
        memory:
          size: 100Mi
        storage:
          size: 100Mi
  placement:
    dcloud:
      pricing:
        osmosis:
          denom: uakt
          amount: 10
deployment:
  osmosis:
    dcloud:
      profile: osmosis
      count: 1
```
